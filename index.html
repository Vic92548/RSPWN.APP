What is the creator program?

The creator program allows any content creator, from any platform to receive 20% of the sales revenues generated by the usage of their creator code and your viewers get 10% off as a reward. So when someone enters your creator code and makes a purchase, you get 20% of the sales revenues directly on your Tebex wallet.

How will I get paid?

Every time someone uses your creator code and makes a purchase on VAPR, you will receive 20% instantly on your Tebex Wallet and you will be able to ask for a PayPal payout right away.

Game Tag System - Automatic Creator Attribution

When you create posts on VAPR, you can now tag games from our catalog. This powerful feature automatically applies your creator code when viewers interact with your content!

How it works:

  1. Tag a VAPR game when creating your post
  2. When viewers click on the game tag in your post, your creator code is automatically applied to their session
  3. If they purchase the game, you earn your 20% commission - no code entry needed!

Pro tip: Always tag the games you're showcasing in your content to maximize your earning potential!

How to join the creator program?

To join the creator program, you just need to have an active VAPR account and a Tebex account. Fill out the form below and we'll review your application. It shouldn't take more than 24 hours in most cases but can take up to a week.

About Dylan

Dylan is a Discord bot designed to help you manage your server efficiently while integrating with VAPR, a platform for sharing and discovering content. With Dylan, you can automate tasks, receive notifications, and interact with your community in new ways.

Available Commands

!setFollowerRole <role-id>

Configure a role to be assigned to followers of a creator.

!notifyPost

Set the current channel to receive notifications for new posts.

!changeLang <lang-code>

Change the bot's language. Supported codes: zh, es, en, hi, ar, bn, pt, ru, ja, pnb, mr, te, wuu, tr, ko, fr, de, vi, ta, yue, ur, jv, it.
